KGP'S GOT TALENT


"By lowering trade barriers through negotiations among member governments, the WTO's system also breaks down other barriers between peoples and trading economies.

ABOUT

KGP's Got Talent is an internship programme, and is part of our mission to help the start up community in IIT Kharagpur.

We target the IITians who crave to be a part of the change, and encourage them to work on real life problems.

They help the campus startups in fast tracking the product development process through the immense talent and skills they possess. Capable interns who are devoted and committed to work and passion get a chance to polish their skills while working with the top minds of the country.

We wish to unearth new talents from within the campus, and boost/headstart the careers of those who participated in the program..

WHO CAN APPLY

This program is open for all B.Tech, M.Tech, LLB, MBA students of IIT Kharagpur who are currently pursuing their degree.

MENTORS

The interns will be guided and mentored by established CEOs/founders, KGP alumni, serial entrepreneurs and professors.
